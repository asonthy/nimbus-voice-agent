# Nimbus Invoice — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus Invoice Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Standard invoice templates
  - Online payment links
  - Basic client management
- **Starter**: $10/user/mo ($8/user/mo billed annually)
  - Custom branding
  - Recurring billing
  - Automated reminders
- **Professional**: $30/user/mo ($24/user/mo billed annually)
  - Client self-service portal
  - Advanced analytics
  - Multi-currency support
  - Custom late fees
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Multiple business entities
  - Advanced security and SSO
  - Dedicated support
  - Custom integration support
Add-ons:
- Advanced Custom Domain: $15 / month — Host your client payment portal on your own custom web address.
