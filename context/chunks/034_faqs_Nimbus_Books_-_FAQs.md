# Nimbus Books — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Books FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Books cost?
A: Nimbus Books starts with a Free tier for basic needs, while our Starter plan is $15 per user per month. Professional is $40 per user per month, and Enterprise pricing is customized to your needs.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial so you can explore the advanced features before committing.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Books?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied within your first 30 days of a paid subscription, we will issue a full refund.
Q: Can I invite my external accountant to Nimbus Books?
A: Yes, all paid plans include a dedicated accountant role that allows your CPA to access your books without consuming a paid user seat.
