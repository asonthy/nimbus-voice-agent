# Nimbus Invoice — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Invoice FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Invoice cost?
A: We offer a Free tier for up to 10 invoices a month. The Starter plan is $10 per user per month, Professional is $30 per user per month, and Enterprise is custom priced.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can try any premium plan free for 14 days with no credit card required.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Invoice?
A: We stand by our 30-day money-back guarantee. If you cancel within the first 30 days of your paid subscription, we will refund your payment in full.
Q: Does Nimbus Invoice charge transaction fees?
A: Nimbus does not charge any additional transaction fees, but standard processing rates from your connected payment gateway will still apply.
