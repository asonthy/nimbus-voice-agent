# Nimbus Vault — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Vault FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Vault cost?
A: Nimbus Vault offers a Free tier for up to 3 users, a Starter tier at 10 USD per user per month, a Professional tier at 25 USD per user per month, and custom Enterprise pricing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Starter and Professional tiers so you can test all premium features.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Vault?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans if you are not completely satisfied.
Q: Can I recover my data if I lose my master password?
A: Because Nimbus Vault uses zero-knowledge encryption, we cannot recover your master password, but administrators can configure account recovery policies for managed users.
