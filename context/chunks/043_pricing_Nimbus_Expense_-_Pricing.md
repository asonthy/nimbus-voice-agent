Source: pricing

Nimbus Expense Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Manual receipt entry
  - Basic expense reports
  - Single-step approvals
- **Starter**: $15/user/mo ($12/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited receipt scanning
  - Virtual corporate cards
  - Mileage tracking
- **Professional**: $35/user/mo ($28/user/mo billed annually)
  - Physical corporate cards
  - Multi-stage approval routing
  - Automated policy enforcement
  - Next-day ACH reimbursements
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Global reimbursement support
  - Advanced security and SSO
  - Dedicated support
  - Custom ERP integrations
