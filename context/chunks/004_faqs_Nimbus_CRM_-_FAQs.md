# Nimbus CRM — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus CRM FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus CRM cost?
A: Nimbus CRM starts with a Free tier for small teams, while paid plans range from $15 per user monthly for Starter to $45 per user monthly for Professional. Enterprise pricing is custom.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all paid plans with no credit card required.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus CRM?
A: We provide a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions if you are not completely satisfied.
Q: Can I import my existing contacts?
A: Absolutely, you can easily import contacts from CSV files or directly from other popular CRM platforms using our migration wizard.
