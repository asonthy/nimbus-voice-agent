# Nimbus Social — Features
Source: products

Nimbus Social Key Features:
- Unified social media publishing calendar
- Bulk scheduling and automated queue management
- Multi-network support including LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook
- Social listening and brand mention monitoring
- Sentiment analysis for customer feedback
- Collaborative drafting and approval workflows
- Unified social inbox for responding to messages
- Customizable analytics and performance reports
- Competitor tracking and benchmarking
