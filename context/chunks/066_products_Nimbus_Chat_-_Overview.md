# Nimbus Chat — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Chat (Customer Support)
Tagline: Live chat and AI bots for instant support.
Summary: Nimbus Chat helps you engage website visitors in real time with proactive messaging and intelligent chatbots.
Nimbus Chat connects you with your customers exactly when they need help. By embedding our lightweight, customizable widget on your website or mobile app, your team can offer instant assistance, answer product questions, and guide users through the checkout process. Proactive triggers allow you to initiate conversations based on user behavior, reducing bounce rates and increasing conversion.

The integrated AI chatbot can handle common questions automatically, freeing up your human agents to focus on complex technical issues. When a human touch is required, the bot seamlessly transfers the conversation to an available agent along with the full chat history and user context, ensuring a frictionless support experience.
