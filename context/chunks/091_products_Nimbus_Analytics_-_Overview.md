# Nimbus Analytics — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Analytics (Analytics & BI)
Tagline: Self-serve business intelligence and reporting made easy.
Summary: Nimbus Analytics empowers your team to explore data, build custom reports, and uncover actionable insights without writing code.
Nimbus Analytics transforms raw data into meaningful business intelligence. Users can drag and drop fields to create comprehensive reports, visualize trends, and share findings across the organization seamlessly.

With native connections to the entire Nimbus suite and external data sources, you can blend sales, marketing, and finance data into a single source of truth. Automated scheduled reports ensure stakeholders always have the latest metrics in their inbox.
