# Nimbus Payroll — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Payroll FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Payroll cost?
A: Nimbus Payroll starts with a Free tier for a few contractors, a Starter plan at $20 per user per month, and a Professional plan at $50 per user per month. Enterprise pricing is custom.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can run simulated payrolls in our 14-day free trial before connecting your actual bank accounts.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Payroll?
A: We provide a 30-day money-back guarantee on all subscription fees. If you are unhappy within the first 30 days, we will refund your software fees.
Q: Does Nimbus Payroll handle tax filings automatically?
A: Yes, on Starter and higher plans, we automatically calculate, withhold, and file your local, state, and federal payroll taxes.
