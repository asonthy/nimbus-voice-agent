# Nimbus Desk — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Desk (Customer Support)
Tagline: Omnichannel helpdesk with powerful SLA management.
Summary: Nimbus Desk brings all your customer conversations into a unified inbox with robust ticketing and automation.
Nimbus Desk allows your support team to manage customer inquiries from email, social media, phone, and web forms in a single interface. By eliminating the need to switch between different applications, agents can resolve issues faster and maintain a consistent customer experience across all channels.

Advanced automation rules let you route tickets to the right agents based on skill, workload, or customer tier. You can easily configure complex service level agreements to ensure timely responses, while detailed reporting dashboards give managers full visibility into team performance and bottlenecks.
