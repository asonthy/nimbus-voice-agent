# Nimbus Recruit — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus Recruit Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - 1 active job posting
  - Basic candidate tracking
  - Standard email templates
- **Starter**: $25/user/mo ($20/user/mo billed annually)
  - 5 active job postings
  - Interview scheduling
  - Custom pipelines
  - Job board syndication
- **Professional**: $60/user/mo ($48/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited job postings
  - Interview kits and scorecards
  - Automated triggers
  - Offer management
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Nimbus SSO support
  - Advanced security controls
  - Dedicated support team
  - Custom recruitment workflows
Add-ons:
- AI Sourcing Assistant: $49 / month — Automatically discover and engage passive candidates matching your job descriptions.
