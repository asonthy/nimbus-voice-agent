- **Free**: Free

  - Bulk scheduling
  - Keyword listening
- **Professional**: $50/user/mo ($40/user/mo billed annually)
  - Approval workflows
  - Sentiment analysis

- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Unlimited social profiles
  - Custom reporting
  - Advanced security
  - Dedicated support
