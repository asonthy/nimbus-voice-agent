# Nimbus CRM — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus CRM Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Basic contact management
  - 1 sales pipeline
  - Standard support
- **Starter**: $15/user/mo ($12/user/mo billed annually)
  - Up to 5 pipelines
  - Custom fields
  - Advanced filters
- **Professional**: $45/user/mo ($36/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited pipelines
  - Revenue forecasting
  - Workflow automation
  - Custom reporting
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Nimbus SSO included
  - Advanced security controls
  - Dedicated support manager
  - Custom onboarding
Add-ons:
- Extra Storage: $10 / 100GB / month — Add additional file storage for customer documents and attachments.
