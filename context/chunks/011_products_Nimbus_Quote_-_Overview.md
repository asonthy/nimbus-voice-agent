# Nimbus Quote — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Quote (Sales & CRM)
Tagline: Create proposals and close deals with ease.
Summary: Nimbus Quote is a complete CPQ and proposal solution that accelerates your sales cycle. Generate accurate quotes, collaborate in deal rooms, and capture secure e-signatures.
Nimbus Quote streamlines the final stages of your sales process by eliminating manual errors and slow approval cycles. Sales reps can quickly configure complex products, apply approved discounts, and generate beautiful, branded proposals in minutes. The platform ensures pricing accuracy while giving leadership full control over discount workflows and margin requirements.

Beyond basic quoting, the software provides interactive deal rooms where buyers and sellers can collaborate, review documents, and negotiate terms in real time. Once an agreement is reached, legally binding e-signatures allow customers to sign off instantly from any device, drastically reducing the time it takes to close business.
