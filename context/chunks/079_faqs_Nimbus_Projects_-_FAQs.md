# Nimbus Projects — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Projects FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Projects cost?
A: We offer a Free tier for small teams, a Starter plan at $10 per user per month, a Professional plan at $30 per user per month, and custom Enterprise pricing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial so you can explore the advanced features before committing.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Projects?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied within your first month, we will refund your payment in full.
Q: Can I track billable hours directly in the app?
A: Absolutely. Nimbus Projects includes native time tracking that syncs directly with Nimbus Invoice for seamless client billing.
