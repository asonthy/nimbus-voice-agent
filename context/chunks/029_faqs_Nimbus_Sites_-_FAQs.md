# Nimbus Sites — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Sites FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Sites cost?
A: Pricing starts with a Free tier, moves to $25 per user per month for Starter, $60 for Professional, and custom pricing for Enterprise.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, we provide a 14-day free trial on all premium plans so you can test the builder before buying.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Sites?
A: All paid plans are backed by our standard 30-day money-back guarantee.
Q: Can I use my own custom domain?
A: Yes, custom domain mapping with free SSL certificates is included on all paid plans.
