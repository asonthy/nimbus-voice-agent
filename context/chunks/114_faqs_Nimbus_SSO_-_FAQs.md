# Nimbus SSO — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus SSO FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus SSO cost?
A: We have a Free tier for small teams, Starter at 15 USD per user per month, Professional at 35 USD per user per month, and custom Enterprise plans.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can evaluate the Professional tier features with a full 14-day free trial.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus SSO?
A: All paid plans are backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.
Q: Does Nimbus SSO support custom SAML applications?
A: Yes, you can easily configure custom SAML 2.0 and OIDC connections for any internal or third-party application.
