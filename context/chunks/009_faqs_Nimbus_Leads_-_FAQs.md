Nimbus Leads FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Leads cost?
A: Nimbus Leads has a Free tier, a Starter plan at $25 per user monthly, a Professional plan at $65 per user monthly, and custom Enterprise pricing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can try Nimbus Leads free for 14 days with full access to Professional features.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Leads?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all initial subscriptions.
Q: Does the dialer support international calling?
A: Yes, our built-in dialer supports calling to over 100 countries, with rates varying by destination region.
