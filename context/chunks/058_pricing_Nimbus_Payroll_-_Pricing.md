# Nimbus Payroll — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus Payroll Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Up to 3 contractors
  - Basic payroll calculations
  - Manual tax reports
- **Starter**: $20/user/mo ($16/user/mo billed annually)
  - Up to 20 employees
  - Automated payroll runs
  - Direct deposit
  - Basic tax filing
- **Professional**: $50/user/mo ($40/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited employees
  - Multi-state tax filing
  - W-2 and 1099 generation
  - General ledger sync
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Nimbus SSO
  - Advanced security and permissions
  - Dedicated payroll specialist
  - Custom integrations
Add-ons:
- International Contractor Payments: $15 / contractor / month — Pay international contractors in their local currency across 120 countries.
