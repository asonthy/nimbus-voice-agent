# Nimbus Leads — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus Leads Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Basic email tracking
  - Manual task queues
  - Standard support
- **Starter**: $25/user/mo ($20/user/mo billed annually)
  - Basic outreach sequences
  - Standard dialer
  - Email templates
- **Professional**: $65/user/mo ($52/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited sequences
  - Power dialer with local presence
  - Predictive lead scoring
  - Call recording
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Nimbus SSO integration
  - Advanced security & compliance
  - Dedicated support and coaching
  - Custom AI scoring models
Add-ons:
- Additional Call Minutes: $15 / 1000 minutes — Purchase extra VoIP calling minutes for high-volume outbound teams.
