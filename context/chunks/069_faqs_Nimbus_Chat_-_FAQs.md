# Nimbus Chat — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Chat FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Chat cost?
A: The Free tier is available at no cost. Paid tiers include Starter at $15 per user per month, and Professional at $45 per user per month. Enterprise plans require custom pricing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can try the Professional plan features free for 14 days without entering a credit card.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Chat?
A: All paid plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you cancel within the first 30 days, you will receive a full refund.
Q: Can I customize the look of the chat widget?
A: Absolutely. You can change the colors, logos, launcher icons, and welcome messages to perfectly match your brand identity.
