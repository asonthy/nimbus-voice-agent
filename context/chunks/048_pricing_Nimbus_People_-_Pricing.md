# Nimbus People — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus People Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Up to 10 employees
  - Basic employee records
  - Time-off requests
- **Starter**: $10/user/mo ($8/user/mo billed annually)
  - Up to 50 employees
  - Custom time-off policies
  - Org charts
  - Basic onboarding
- **Professional**: $30/user/mo ($24/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited employees
  - Performance reviews
  - Advanced reporting
  - E-signatures
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Nimbus SSO integration
  - Advanced security and compliance
  - Dedicated support manager
  - Custom HR workflows
Add-ons:
- Advanced Analytics: $5 / user / month — Gain deeper insights into employee retention, diversity metrics, and compensation trends.
