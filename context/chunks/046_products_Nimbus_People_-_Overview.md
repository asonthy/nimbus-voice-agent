# Nimbus People — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus People (HR & People)
Tagline: Centralize your workforce and streamline employee management.
Summary: Nimbus People is a comprehensive HRIS that manages your entire employee lifecycle from onboarding to offboarding. Keep your organization aligned with centralized records, time off tracking, and interactive org charts.
Managing employee data shouldn't be a hassle. Nimbus People gives your HR team a single source of truth for employee records, performance evaluations, and document management. Your staff can easily request time off, view company holidays, and update their personal information through an intuitive self-service portal.

Beyond basic records, Nimbus People helps you build a strong company culture. Automated onboarding workflows ensure new hires feel welcome and prepared from day one. Interactive org charts and employee directories make it simple for teams to connect and collaborate across different departments and locations.
