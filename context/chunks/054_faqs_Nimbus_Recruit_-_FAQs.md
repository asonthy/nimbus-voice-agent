# Nimbus Recruit — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Recruit FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Recruit cost?
A: We offer a Free tier for basic needs, a Starter plan at $25 per user per month, and a Professional plan at $60 per user per month. Enterprise plans are custom priced based on your organization.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can try the Starter or Professional plans free for 14 days, no credit card required.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Recruit?
A: All paid plans are backed by our 30-day money-back guarantee. If you cancel within the first 30 days, we will issue a full refund.
Q: Can I post to multiple job boards at once?
A: Absolutely. Nimbus Recruit allows you to syndicate your job postings to over 50 major job boards with a single click.
