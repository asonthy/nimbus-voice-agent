# Nimbus Dashboards — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus Dashboards Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - 1 dashboard
  - Standard widgets
  - Daily refresh
- **Starter**: $10/user/mo ($8/user/mo billed annually)
  - 10 dashboards
  - Hourly refresh
  - Basic alerts
  - TV mode
- **Professional**: $30/user/mo ($24/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited dashboards
  - Minute-by-minute refresh
  - Advanced threshold alerts
  - Custom themes
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - SSO
  - Advanced security
  - Dedicated support
  - White-labeling
Add-ons:
- Extra Alert Credits: $5 / month — Add 500 additional SMS or webhook alerts to your monthly quota.
