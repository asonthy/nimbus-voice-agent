# Nimbus Boards — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus Boards Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - 3 whiteboards
  - Basic kanban features
  - Standard shapes
- **Starter**: $12/user/mo ($9/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited boards
  - Custom card fields
  - Board templates
- **Professional**: $25/user/mo ($20/user/mo billed annually)
  - Advanced automations
  - Voting and polling
  - WIP limits
  - Cross-board reporting
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - SSO and provisioning
  - Advanced security
  - Dedicated support
  - Admin board controls
