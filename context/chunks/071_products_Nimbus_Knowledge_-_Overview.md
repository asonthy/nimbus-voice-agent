# Nimbus Knowledge — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Knowledge (Customer Support)
Tagline: Empower customers with self service help centers.
Summary: Nimbus Knowledge lets you build beautiful, searchable help centers so customers can find answers on their own.
Nimbus Knowledge provides an intuitive authoring environment for creating, organizing, and publishing support articles, tutorials, and FAQs. By building a comprehensive self service portal, you can significantly reduce the volume of incoming support tickets and provide your customers with 24/7 access to the information they need. The platform supports rich media, allowing you to embed videos, images, and code snippets directly into your documentation.

Beyond content creation, Nimbus Knowledge offers powerful analytics to help you understand what your customers are searching for. You can track article views, analyze failed search queries, and collect feedback ratings to continuously improve your content. The fully customizable portal can be hosted on your own custom domain and styled to match your corporate website seamlessly.
