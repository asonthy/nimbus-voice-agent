# Nimbus Leads — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Leads (Sales & CRM)
Tagline: Automate outreach and engage your best prospects.
Summary: Nimbus Leads empowers your sales development reps with automated outreach sequences, a built-in dialer, and intelligent lead scoring. Connect with the right prospects at the perfect time.
Nimbus Leads transforms how your team handles outbound sales by automating repetitive outreach tasks. You can create multi-channel sequences that combine emails, phone calls, and social touches into a seamless workflow. The built-in power dialer allows reps to call through lists rapidly while automatically logging call notes and outcomes directly into your database.

To ensure your team focuses on the highest value targets, the platform includes predictive lead scoring based on engagement metrics and demographic data. Real-time analytics help managers coach their teams effectively, optimize messaging, and maximize connection rates across all communication channels.
