# Nimbus Social — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Social FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Social cost?
A: Nimbus Social includes a Free tier, a Starter plan for $20 per user per month, a Professional plan for $50 per user per month, and custom Enterprise packages.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can try the Professional tier features free for 14 days without entering a credit card.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Social?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you are not completely satisfied with your subscription.
Q: Which social networks do you support?
A: We currently support scheduling and monitoring for Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest, TikTok, and YouTube.
