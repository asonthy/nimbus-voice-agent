# Nimbus Desk — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Desk FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Desk cost?
A: Nimbus Desk offers a Free tier for up to 3 users. Paid plans start at $20 per user per month on the Starter tier, and $50 per user per month on the Professional tier. Enterprise pricing is custom.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan so you can test all the advanced features before committing.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Desk?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied within your first 30 days of a paid subscription, you can request a full refund.
Q: Can I merge duplicate tickets?
A: Yes, Nimbus Desk allows agents to easily merge duplicate tickets from the same customer to keep the conversation history clean and organized.
