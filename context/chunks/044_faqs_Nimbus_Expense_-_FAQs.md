# Nimbus Expense — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Expense FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Expense cost?
A: Nimbus Expense is free for basic receipt tracking. Starter is $15 per user per month, Professional is $35 per user per month, and Enterprise is custom priced based on your organization size.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Absolutely, you can test drive the Starter or Professional plans free for 14 days.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Expense?
A: All our paid plans are backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee. If it is not a fit, cancel within 30 days for a complete refund.
Q: Are the corporate cards credit or prepaid cards?
A: Nimbus corporate cards are charge cards that draw directly from your connected business bank account, meaning no interest charges or credit checks are required.
