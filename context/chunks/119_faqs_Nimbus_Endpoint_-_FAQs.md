Nimbus Endpoint FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Endpoint cost?
A: Pricing starts with a Free tier, moves to Starter at 20 USD per user per month, Professional at 45 USD per user per month, and Enterprise for custom needs.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on our Starter and Professional plans.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Endpoint?
A: We stand by our product with a full 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions.
Q: Will the endpoint agent slow down employee computers?
A: No, our agent is designed to be ultra-lightweight and consumes minimal system resources during background scans.
