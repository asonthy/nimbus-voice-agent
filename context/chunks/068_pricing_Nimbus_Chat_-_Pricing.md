Source: pricing

Nimbus Chat Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Basic chat widget
  - Standard chat history
  - 1 active trigger
- **Starter**: $15/user/mo ($12/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited chat history
  - Proactive triggers
  - Pre-chat forms
  - Visitor tracking
- **Professional**: $45/user/mo ($36/user/mo billed annually)
  - AI chatbot builder
  - Advanced routing
  - Multiple widgets
  - Chat analytics
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - Nimbus SSO
  - Custom AI model training
  - Dedicated support
  - Advanced security compliance
Add-ons:
- Extra Bot Sessions: $25 / 1000 sessions / month — Add more automated AI chatbot sessions to your monthly limit.
