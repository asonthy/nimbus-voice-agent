# Nimbus People — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus People FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus People cost?
A: Nimbus People offers a Free tier for small teams, a Starter plan at $10 per user per month, and a Professional plan at $30 per user per month. Enterprise pricing is custom and available upon request.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial so you can explore the premium features before committing.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus People?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied within the first 30 days of your paid subscription, you can request a full refund.
Q: Can employees update their own information?
A: Yes, Nimbus People includes a self-service portal where employees can safely update personal details, request time off, and access tax documents.
