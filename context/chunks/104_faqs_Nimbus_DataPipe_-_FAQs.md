Nimbus DataPipe FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus DataPipe cost?
A: We offer a Free tier, a Starter plan at $25 per user per month, a Professional plan at $65 per user per month, and Enterprise pricing upon request.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, new users can explore all Professional features with a 14-day free trial.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus DataPipe?
A: All paid plans are backed by our 30-day money-back guarantee for your peace of mind.
Q: Which data warehouses are supported?
A: We currently support Amazon Redshift, Google BigQuery, Snowflake, and PostgreSQL.
