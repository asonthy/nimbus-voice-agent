Nimbus Dashboards FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Dashboards cost?
A: Pricing starts with a Free tier, followed by Starter at $10 per user per month, Professional at $30 per user per month, and custom Enterprise plans.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, you can try the Professional plan features free for 14 days.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Dashboards?
A: We provide a complete 30-day money-back guarantee on all initial paid subscriptions.
Q: Can I display these dashboards on office TVs?
A: Absolutely, our dedicated TV mode optimizes the layout for large screens and automatically cycles through multiple dashboard tabs.
