# Nimbus Payroll — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Payroll (HR & People)
Tagline: Accurate, automated payroll and tax filing simplified.
Summary: Nimbus Payroll takes the complexity out of paying your team by automating payroll runs, tax calculations, and compliance. Easily pay full-time employees and independent contractors in a few clicks.
Processing payroll is critical, and accuracy is non-negotiable. Nimbus Payroll automates the entire payroll lifecycle, calculating wages, deductions, and taxes with precision. You can set payroll to run automatically on your preferred schedule, ensuring your team gets paid on time, every time, via direct deposit or printed checks.

Staying compliant with local and federal tax regulations is effortless. The platform automatically files your payroll taxes and generates necessary year-end forms like W-2s and 1099s. With support for both salaried employees and hourly contractors, Nimbus Payroll adapts to the unique structure of your modern workforce.
