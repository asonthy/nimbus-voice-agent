# Nimbus Projects — Pricing
Source: pricing

Nimbus Projects Pricing Tiers:
- **Free**: Free
  - Basic task management
  - List and board views
  - Up to 3 projects
- **Starter**: $10/user/mo ($8/user/mo billed annually)
  - Unlimited projects
  - Gantt charts
  - Basic reporting
- **Professional**: $30/user/mo ($24/user/mo billed annually)
  - Resource management
  - Time tracking
  - Portfolio views
  - Custom workflows
- **Enterprise**: Custom / Contact sales
  - SSO integration
  - Advanced security
  - Dedicated support
  - Custom data retention
Add-ons:
- Premium Client Portals: $49 / month — White-labeled project portals for your external clients.
