# Nimbus Knowledge — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Knowledge FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Knowledge cost?
A: Nimbus Knowledge is Free for up to 3 users. The Starter plan is $15 per user per month, and the Professional plan is $35 per user per month. Enterprise pricing is tailored to your needs.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, a 14-day free trial is available for the Professional tier to let you explore custom domains and advanced analytics.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Knowledge?
A: We stand by our product with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are unhappy during your first month of a paid plan, we will refund your payment.
Q: Can I host the help center on my own domain?
A: Yes, starting from the Starter plan, you can map your knowledge base to a custom domain or subdomain of your choosing.
