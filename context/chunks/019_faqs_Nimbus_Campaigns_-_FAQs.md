# Nimbus Campaigns — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Campaigns FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Campaigns cost?
A: We offer a Free tier for basic needs, a Starter plan at $15 per user per month, a Professional plan at $45 per user per month, and custom Enterprise pricing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on our Starter and Professional plans with no credit card required.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Campaigns?
A: We stand by our product with a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new paid subscriptions.
Q: Can I import my existing contact lists?
A: Absolutely, you can easily import contacts via CSV or sync them directly from Nimbus CRM and other supported integrations.
