# Nimbus Recruit — Overview
Source: products

Nimbus Recruit (HR & People)
Tagline: Attract, evaluate, and hire top talent faster.
Summary: Nimbus Recruit is a modern applicant tracking system designed to streamline your hiring process. Create custom hiring pipelines, schedule interviews, and collaborate with your team to find the perfect candidates.
Finding the right talent requires a structured and efficient hiring process. Nimbus Recruit provides a central hub where recruiters and hiring managers can track applicants across multiple job postings. You can easily build custom hiring pipelines, automate email communications, and parse resumes with intelligent data extraction to save time on manual entry.

Collaboration is at the heart of great hiring decisions. With built-in interview kits, scorecards, and feedback loops, your hiring team can evaluate candidates objectively and consistently. Seamless integrations with major job boards ensure your postings reach a wide audience, while detailed analytics help you identify bottlenecks in your recruitment funnel.
