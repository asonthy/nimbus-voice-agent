# Nimbus Boards — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Boards FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Boards cost?
A: Pricing ranges from a Free tier to $12 per user per month for Starter, $25 for Professional, and custom Enterprise pricing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, sign up for a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan to test all premium features.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Boards?
A: All paid plans are backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee.
Q: Can I link kanban cards to Nimbus Projects tasks?
A: Yes, Nimbus Boards integrates natively with Nimbus Projects, allowing you to sync cards and tasks bidirectionally.
