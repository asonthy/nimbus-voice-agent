# Nimbus Analytics — FAQs
Source: faqs

Nimbus Analytics FAQs:
Q: What does Nimbus Analytics cost?
A: We offer a Free tier for basic needs, a Starter plan at $15 per user per month, a Professional plan at $45 per user per month, and custom Enterprise pricing.
Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial so you can test our advanced features.
Q: What is the refund policy for Nimbus Analytics?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you are not completely satisfied with your purchase.
Q: Can I embed reports in my own application?
A: Yes, embedded analytics is available on the Professional and Enterprise plans.
